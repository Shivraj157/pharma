Pharmacy Companies & Leaders — Directory

A clean, printable directory of 30 pharma companies with founder/family background, current leader, education, and a short summary. Images are placeholders so you can swap in real photos/logos later.

Sun Pharmaceuticals

Founded: 1983 HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dilip Shanghvi, whose family ran a small pharma distribution business in Kolkata.

Current Leader: Dilip Shanghvi (Executive Chairman/Founder).

Education (Leader): B.Com, University of Calcutta.

Summary: Grew from a psychiatry-drug maker into India’s largest pharma via focused specialty strategy and acquisitions (Taro, Ranbaxy).

Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories

Founded: 1984 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dr. K. Anji Reddy; the company remains promoter-led by the Reddy family.

Current Leader: Kallam Satish Reddy (Chairman).

Education (Leader): Chemical Engineering; advanced studies in the United States.

Summary: Transitioned from APIs to global finished dosages; strong presence in the US generics market and proprietary products.

Cipla

Founded: 1935 HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Khwaja Abdul Hamied; later led by his son Yusuf K. Hamied with a mission of affordable medicines.

Current Leader: Dr. Yusuf Khwaja Hamied (Chairman Emeritus/Chairman).

Education (Leader): PhD in Chemistry, University of Cambridge.

Summary: Pioneered low-cost access to essential drugs (notably antiretrovirals), shaping global access-to-medicines debates.

Biocon

Founded: 1978 HQ: Bengaluru

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Kiran Mazumdar‑Shaw, who began in a garage and built a biotech pioneer.

Current Leader: Kiran Mazumdar‑Shaw (Executive Chairperson).

Education (Leader): B.Sc. (Zoology), Bangalore University; Master Brewer, Ballarat (Australia).

Summary: Grew from enzymes to biosimilars and novel biologics with a focus on affordable innovation.

Torrent Pharmaceuticals

Founded: 1959/1971 HQ: Ahmedabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by U. N. Mehta; now led by the Mehta family.

Current Leader: Samir Mehta (Chairman).

Education (Leader): Business degree (B.K. School of Business Management, Ahmedabad).

Summary: Scaled via acquisitions (Elder Pharma, Unichem, Curatio) and strong India brands with global reach.

Lupin

Founded: 1968 HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Desh Bandhu Gupta; promoter family remains active in leadership.

Current Leaders: Vinita D. Gupta (CEO) and Nilesh Gupta (MD).

Education (Leaders): Vinita: B.Pharm (Mumbai), MBA (Kellogg); Nilesh: Chem Engg (UDCT), MBA (Wharton).

Summary: Expanded aggressively in the US generics market and complex generics; strong presence in CV, diabetes, respiratory.

Glenmark Pharmaceuticals

Founded: 1977 HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Gracias Saldanha; named from sons Glenn & Mark.

Current Leader: Glenn Saldanha (Chairman & MD).

Education (Leader): B.Pharm (Mumbai); MBA (NYU Stern).

Summary: Evolved from generics to innovation-led R&D in respiratory, dermatology, oncology with global partnerships.

Wockhardt

Founded: 1960s HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dr. Habil Khorakiwala; family remains in management.

Current Leadership: Promoter family leadership, including MD roles by the next generation.

Education (Founder): Pharmacy/business education (India/US).

Summary: Known for antibiotics and insulin, with operations across India, UK, and emerging markets.

Zydus Lifesciences (Cadila Healthcare)

Founded: 1952 HQ: Ahmedabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Ramanbhai Patel; Patel family continues to lead.

Current Leader: Pankaj R. Patel (Chairman).

Education (Leader): B.Pharm & M.Pharm, L.M. College of Pharmacy.

Summary: Broad portfolio of generics, vaccines (e.g., ZyCoV‑D), biosimilars, and consumer products.

Aurobindo Pharma

Founded: 1986 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Co‑founded by P. V. Ramprasad Reddy and K. Nithyananda Reddy.

Current Leader: Promoter leadership including the Reddy family.

Education (Founder): Commerce background (P.V.R. Reddy); engineering/sciences within team.

Summary: Among India’s top exporters of APIs and generics with strong US/EU filings.

Piramal Pharma / Piramal Enterprises

Founded: 1980s (group origins earlier) HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Family Background: Led by Ajay Piramal; family diversified group with pharma/financial services.

Current Leader: Ajay Piramal (Chairman); Nandini Piramal (leadership in pharma unit).

Education (Leader): Ajay Piramal: Masters in Management Studies (JBIMS, Mumbai).

Summary: Known for CRAMS/CDMO, critical care, and complex generics; portfolio reshaped via demergers and M&A.

Alembic Pharmaceuticals

Founded: 1907 (group origins) HQ: Vadodara

Founder / Family Background: Part of Alembic Group; Amin family are key promoters.

Current Leader: Chirayu R. Amin (Chairman).

Education (Leader): Business/industrial background.

Summary: Legacy Indian pharma with US generics, APIs, and specialty focus in selected therapies.

Jubilant Pharmova

Founded: 1978 (group origins) HQ: Noida

Founder / Family Background: Founded by brothers Shyam S. Bhartia and Hari S. Bhartia.

Current Leader: Shyam S. Bhartia (Chairman); Hari S. Bhartia (Co‑Chairman).

Education (Leaders): Engineering/management backgrounds.

Summary: Diversified pharma, contract research and manufacturing, and radiopharma services.

Alkem Laboratories

Founded: 1973 HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Samprada Singh; family continues to run the company.

Current Leader: Executive leadership includes Basudeo N. Singh (Chairman Emeritus) and Sandeep Singh (MD).

Education (Leaders): Business/management backgrounds.

Summary: Strong India brands in anti-infectives and pain management with growing US generics footprint.

NATCO Pharma

Founded: 1981 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by V. C. Nannapaneni.

Current Leader: V. C. Nannapaneni (Chairman); Rajeev Nannapaneni (leadership).

Education (Leaders): Science/management backgrounds.

Summary: Focus on oncology and complex generics; notable for compulsory licensing and patent challenges.

IPCA Laboratories

Founded: 1949 HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Family Background: Early promoters included K. B. Mehta; later driven by the Godha family.

Current Leader: Premchand Godha (Chairman & MD).

Education (Leader): Pharmacy/industrial background.

Summary: Strong in anti-malarials and APIs; resilient growth with exports to key markets.

Strides Pharma Science

Founded: 1990 HQ: Bengaluru

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Arun Kumar.

Current Leader: Arun Kumar (Chairman).

Education (Leader): Business background.

Summary: Focus on regulated market generics, soft-gels, and emerging market brands; known for deal-making.

Gland Pharma

Founded: 1978 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by P. V. N. Raju; significant stake later by Fosun Pharma.

Current Leader: Srinivas Sadu (MD & CEO).

Education (Leader): Business/engineering background.

Summary: Strong in sterile injectables and complex formulations, exporting to regulated markets.

Neuland Laboratories

Founded: 1984 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dr. D. R. Rao; promoter family remains active.

Current Leader: Sucheth Davuluri (Vice Chairman & CEO); Saharsh Davuluri (Vice Chairman & MD).

Education (Leaders): Engineering/management backgrounds.

Summary: API specialist with focus on custom synthesis and complex molecules.

Subsidiary of Abbott (US) HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Background: Indian subsidiary of Abbott Laboratories.

Current Leader: Country leadership (Managing Director title varies over time).

Education (Leader): —

Summary: Strong India brand portfolio, especially in gastro, women’s health, and metabolic therapies.

Pfizer India

Subsidiary of Pfizer (US) HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Background: Indian listed subsidiary of Pfizer Inc.

Current Leader: Country President/MD role changes periodically.

Education (Leader): —

Summary: Branded formulations across CV, anti-infectives, and vaccines; imports and local manufacturing.

Subsidiary of Sanofi (France) HQ: Mumbai

Founder / Background: Indian arm of Sanofi group.

Current Leader: India MD/CEO designation varies by year.

Education (Leader): —

Summary: Focus on diabetes care, vaccines, and consumer health brands in India.

Mankind Pharma

Founded: 1991 HQ: New Delhi

Founder / Family Background: Founded by R. C. Juneja (with Rajeev Juneja as co‑promoter).

Current Leaders: R. C. Juneja (Chairman); Rajeev Juneja (Vice Chairman & MD).

Education (Leaders): Business/industry backgrounds.

Summary: Built some of India’s biggest brands across acute and chronic therapies; strong domestic focus with growing exports.

Divi’s Laboratories

Founded: 1990 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dr. Murali K. Divi, a chemist and entrepreneur.

Current Leader: Dr. Murali K. Divi (Chairman & MD).

Education (Leader): Chemistry/pharmaceutical sciences background.

Summary: Global leader in APIs and custom synthesis with world‑scale facilities.

Bharat Biotech

Founded: 1996 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dr. Krishna M. Ella and Suchitra Ella.

Current Leader: Dr. Krishna M. Ella (Chairman); Suchitra Ella (Co‑founder & leader).

Education (Leader): PhD (Univ. of Wisconsin–Madison) in plant pathology/biotechnology.

Summary: Known for vaccines and biologics; developed Covaxin with ICMR/NIV.

Serum Institute of India

Founded: 1966 HQ: Pune

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dr. Cyrus S. Poonawalla; family continues to run the group.

Current Leader: Adar Poonawalla (CEO).

Education (Leader): Business studies (UK).

Summary: World’s largest vaccine manufacturer by doses; produced Covishield (AstraZeneca/Oxford) for India and many countries.

Hetero

Founded: 1993 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dr. B. Partha Saradhi Reddy.

Current Leader: Dr. B. P. S. Reddy (Chairman).

Education (Leader): Doctorate in chemistry/pharmaceutical sciences.

Summary: One of India’s largest API and generic drug manufacturers with strong ARV and oncology portfolios.

Granules India

Founded: 1984/1991 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Krishna Prasad Chigurupati.

Current Leader: K. P. Chigurupati (Chairman & MD).

Education (Leader): Science/management background.

Summary: Known for large‑scale paracetamol/ibuprofen value chain and finished dose manufacturing.

Laurus Labs

Founded: 2005 HQ: Hyderabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Dr. Satyanarayana Chava.

Current Leader: Dr. Satyanarayana Chava (Founder & CEO/VCMD).

Education (Leader): PhD/advanced chemistry background.

Summary: Focus on APIs, formulations, and biotech (Laurus Bio) with global regulatory approvals.

Intas Pharmaceuticals

Founded: 1970s HQ: Ahmedabad

Founder / Family Background: Founded by Hasmukh Chudgar; Chudgar family continues to lead.

Current Leaders: Hasmukh Chudgar (Chairman); Nimish Chudgar (MD/CEO roles).
